Go to settings.

Acquirer

On the payment page, select the Qpay Payment Acquirer and click on Pay Now button.

Make Payment

After clicking on Pay Now, you will be redirected to Qpay payment page. Enter the details to make the payment

Order Confirmed

Once the Payment is made the order is confirmed.
